About the Club

Adventure is better with a crew.

LA Adventure Club started with a simple idea: there's a whole coastline, mountain range, and set of canyons right here in our backyard — and it's a lot more fun to explore them with good people than alone.

We run casual, beginner-friendly trips a couple times a month: paddle boarding the harbors and canals, hiking the San Gabriels, and the occasional island weekend. No membership fees, no cliques, no pressure to be hardcore. Show up, get outside, make some friends, and we'll film it so you've got something to look back on.
